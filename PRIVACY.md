# Privacy Policy — RachtaZ Stream Enhancer

_Last updated: 2026_

**RachtaZ Stream Enhancer** is a free, fan-made browser extension.

## Data collected

**None.** The extension does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

## How it works

- The extension reads the content of YouTube watch pages
  (`https://www.youtube.com/watch*`) only to find the timecodes posted in the
  comments and to inject the navigation buttons and markers.
- Detected timecodes and the extension status are stored **locally** in your
  browser (`chrome.storage.local`) to speed up later visits. This data never
  leaves your device and can be erased by removing the extension.

## No third-party servers

The extension does not communicate with any server. It contains no trackers, no
ads, and no analytics.
